About

Hello, I'm Linda.

A little over a year ago, I decided to teach myself data science.

I started by taking Harvard's CS50 course after enrolling online in edx.

That course was the beginning of what I hope will be a lifelong journey in this field.

Taking that course made me realize that I could learn anything I wanted to as long as I had access.

Access to the internet.

Access to information.

As long as I have access and I put my mind to it, I can learn anything I wish to learn.

The democratization of education has made learning accessible to willing students all over the world, and I am a testament to that.
